## 📈 How to Use This Checklist

1. **Start Small**: Pick 3-5 tools that solve your immediate pain points
2. **Try Before You Commit**: Most tools offer free trials or tiers
3. **Integrate Gradually**: Don't try to adopt everything at once
4. **Share With Your Team**: Standardize tools for better collaboration
5. **Revisit Quarterly**: Remove unused tools, add new ones
