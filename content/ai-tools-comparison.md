**Best for:** Marketing teams, agencies, long-form content

- Superior long-form quality
- Excellent SEO integration
- Robust team features
- Brand voice training
- Active community

**Weaknesses:**
- No free plan
- Expensive for individuals
- Learning curve

**Verdict:** Premium choice for serious content operations

### Copy.ai ($36/mo)

**Best for:** Marketing copy, social media, emails

**Strengths:**
- Generous free plan (2,000 words)
- 90+ templates
- Easiest to use
- Great for short-form
- Chrome extension

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited long-form
- Basic SEO features
- Team features lacking

**Verdict:** Best entry point for AI writing

### Writesonic ($16/mo)

**Best for:** SEO blogs, budget-conscious users

**Strengths:**
- Lowest price for quality
- Excellent SEO features
- AI Article Writer
- WordPress integration
- Free plan

**Weaknesses:**
- Interface complexity
- Inconsistent quality
- Limited short-form

**Verdict:** Best value for bloggers

### Rytr ($9/mo)

**Best for:** Budget users, simple content needs

**Strengths:**
- Cheapest premium option
- Simple interface
- Decent quality for price
- Chrome extension

**Weaknesses:**
- Limited advanced features
- Lower output quality
- Fewer templates

**Verdict:** Good starter tool

### Sudowrite ($19/mo)

**Weaknesses:**

### ChatGPT Plus ($20/mo)

**Best for:** General use, research, flexibility

**Weaknesses:**

- No built-in SEO
- Requires prompt engineering

**Verdict:** Swiss Army knife of AI writing
