## Copy.ai Limitations

### 1. Short-Form Focus
Copy.ai struggles with long-form content over 1,000 words. Paragraphs feel disjointed in longer pieces.

**Workaround:** Use the Blog Wizard for structure, then expand manually.

### 2. No SEO Integration
Unlike Jasper's Surfer integration, Copy.ai lacks built-in SEO optimization.

**Workaround:** Use external tools like Clearscope or Surfer separately.

### 3. Repetitive Outputs
After heavy use, you may see similar phrasing across different prompts.

**Workaround:** Use the "More Like This" and "Less Like This" feedback buttons.
