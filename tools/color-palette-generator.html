Color Theory Algorithms

            
Our generator uses proven color theory algorithms to create harmonious palettes:

            
    
                
  • Complementary: Colors opposite each other on the color wheel for high contrast
    • 
                
  • Triadic: Three evenly spaced colors for vibrant, balanced schemes
    • 
                
  • Analogous: Adjacent colors for harmonious, natural combinations
    • 
                
  • Monochromatic: Variations in lightness and saturation of a single hue
    • 
                
  • Split Complementary: One base color plus two adjacent to its complement
    • 
                
  • Tetradic: Four colors in rectangular formation for rich variety

Accessibility First

            
We believe great design should be accessible to everyone. Our built-in WCAG contrast checker helps you create color combinations that are readable for users with visual impairments. The tool checks against WCAG 2.1 AA and AAA standards for both normal and large text.
