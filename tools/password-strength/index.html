Why Password Strength Matters

Weak passwords are the #1 cause of account breaches. Modern computers can test billions of passwords per second.

  • 8 characters: Cracked in minutes
  • 12 characters: Cracked in days/weeks
  • 16+ characters: Takes centuries to crack
  • Each additional character makes your password exponentially stronger.
