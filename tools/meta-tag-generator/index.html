SEO Best Practices

Title Tags

  • Keep titles between 50-60 characters to avoid truncation in search results
  • Include your primary keyword near the beginning
  • Make each page title unique across your site
  • Avoid keyword stuffing - write for humans first

Meta Descriptions

  • Limit to 150-160 characters for optimal display
  • Include a clear call-to-action
  • Use your target keywords naturally
  • Make it compelling to improve click-through rates

Social Sharing

  • Open Graph images should be 1200x630px for best results
  • Twitter Cards work best with images at least 1200x600px
  • Always test your links with Facebook Debugger and Twitter Card Validator
