Why Use Our Word Counter?

Our free word counter tool is designed for writers, bloggers, content creators, and SEO professionals who need quick and accurate text statistics. Whether you're writing an essay, blog post, or social media content, knowing your word count helps you stay within platform limits and optimize for search engines.

Features

  • Real-time counting - Stats update as you type
  • Words & characters - See both with and without spaces
  • Sentences & paragraphs - Understand your text structure
  • Reading time - Estimate how long your content takes to read
  • Privacy-focused - Your text never leaves your browser
  • Mobile-friendly - Works on all devices

Ideal For

  • Writers tracking daily word counts
  • Bloggers optimizing content length for SEO
  • Students checking essay requirements
  • Social media managers staying within character limits
  • Content marketers analyzing readability

SEO Content Length Guidelines

Different content types perform best at different lengths. Here are general guidelines for optimal SEO performance:

  • Social media posts: 40-80 characters for maximum engagement
  • Meta descriptions: 150-160 characters for search results
  • Blog posts: 1,500-2,500 words for comprehensive coverage
  • Product descriptions: 300-500 words for e-commerce
  • Email subject lines: 40-50 characters for best open rates
